product description

The Dream Repair Serum contains 18 powerful ingredients, demonstrating a very high level of anti-oxidant cellular repair. The Beauty Chef's unique Bio-synergistic Blending (™) process blends ingredients to create a super charged formula, rich in anti-oxidants to repair and rejuvenate skin cells during the sleep cycle.

Powerful Vitamin E analogues found in raspberry seed oil and acai berry oil work synergistically with carotenoids/ provitamin A found in tomato fruit and sea buckthorn berry to boost anti-oxidant levels up to 100 times. Scientific studies show that these two constituents co-operate very effectively within the lipid membrane to repair and rejuvenate the cell, magnifying each other's skin enhancing properties.

The lipid soluble constituencies found in the serum such as polyphenols, mixed chain fatty acids and phospholipids provide outstanding emollient skin conditioning, anti-inflammatory and anti-oxidant protection. Also containing vitamins A, B, C, E, K and P and omegas 3,6,7 and 9, this formula is a profound rejuvenator, protecting and synthesising collagen; for a healthier, smoother appearance.
